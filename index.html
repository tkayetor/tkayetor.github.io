About Mia

Hello there, and welcome to my blog! I am so glad you found me. A little bit about me, I am a mom of four awesomely talented, kind, beautiful little girls. Seriously I’m not just saying that because they’re my children….Okay I am, but it’s still true. I also happen to be a wife to veteran Marine who makes life adventurous (that’s putting it mildly). I love them all quite passionately, second only to Jesus my very best friend. You should totally follow Him. Beyond that I’m just your average introverted mom who loves fashion, and is truly intrigued by design, fashion and saving money. I started this blog as my personal “mommy-break.” This is a space where I get to escape and pretend that four sets of eyes are not staring me down…waiting on ___________( you fill in the blank). A space where I get to conquer my fears and share my love for design. This is where I can share my love/hate relationship with “She Who Must not be Tamed” a.k.a my hair. Yes, I named her. I force myself to experience new and old things, like actually being in part of the family photo, or Anne of Green Gables uninterrupted. A mom can dream right? In this place I hope you’ll find that; Life is messy but fun, tragic yet hopeful, and that fairy tales only happen in faraway lands. To find out more about what motivated me to start this blog checkout Be Your Own Kind of Beautiful. It’s my first official blog. Feel free to share with me your own sweet deals or thoughts, and follow me on Instagram where I’ll regularly post any new deals I find including some for the kiddos.
